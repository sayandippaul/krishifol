Welcome to Krishifol — Your Smart Farming Companion! 🌱

Namaste! We are thrilled to have you here. Krishifol is designed with a vision to empower farmers and agriculture enthusiasts with the power of technology. Our platform offers innovative features to enhance your farming experience:


🌾 Crop Recommendation: Find the best crop for your soil based on essential parameters like nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, temperature, pH, and rainfall.


🍎 Fruit Health & Nutrition: Upload an image of a fruit, and Krishifol will identify it and provide valuable nutritional information.


🌿 Disease Detection: Snap a picture of your crop, and our advanced machine learning models will help diagnose potential diseases, offering insights to protect and improve your yield.


At Krishifol, we blend traditional wisdom with modern technology to create a sustainable future for farming. Let’s grow together, one field at a time! 🌾🌍
